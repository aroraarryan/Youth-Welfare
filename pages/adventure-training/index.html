About the Program

Conducted in partnership with the Nehru Institute of Mountaineering (NIM), Uttarkashi (est. 1965), participants undergo structured training in Himalayan adventure sports including trekking, rock climbing, river navigation, and wilderness survival. The program builds physical fitness, mental resilience, leadership, and teamwork.
